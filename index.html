ROI Calculator

Want to know how Marketron REV will deliver ROI for your organization? We’ve created this handy ROI calculator to measure multiple areas of value. Plug in your numbers below to see how REV can boost your revenue and operational efficiency.

Rate Improvement

When using yield management, you can generate higher revenue. Enter your projected or budgeted increase with your current annual revenue. On average, REV adds 5% to your top-line revenue.


Sales Efficiency

Sellers save so much time using REV, and they can sell more advertising each month. On average, sellers have 20%-40% more time. Enter the number of salespeople in your organization and the average deal amount to see how much more per month and year they could deliver.

Pay for Performance

Growing your pay-for-performance (P4P) revenue is critical, and REV helps you do it. Automate and improve the P4P process to grow revenue of current P4P campaigns. Enter the current P4P annual revenue and multiply by P4P industry average growth of 20% to see the return.

Automation for Multi-market and Cross-Channel (Linear and Digital) Proposals

REV makes proposal creation easy and fast, meaning your sellers can close more deals. Enter the approximate number of new multi-market and cross-channel deals you could close and their average value. If unsure, input 12 for one per month.

Operational Efficiency

REV’s dynamic pricing automation eliminates many manual tasks and reduces the need for pre-emptions, make-goods and credits. Having to credit back dollars can add up on a monthly basis. Input the number of stations you have along with the value of the credits per month for each station, to see the total dollars retained by your station group.

Technology Cost Savings

By using REV, you can eliminate other technology costs, such as a media sales platform or standalone yield management tool. Enter the annual fees for these platforms to reveal your savings.
